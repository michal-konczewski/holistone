About the resort

Valley of
limestone.

Holistone Kościelisko occupies a historic valley at the gateway to the Tatra National Park. The landscape is defined by limestone rock formations, highland meadows and the distinct character of Podhale culture.

The resort draws on highland architectural tradition — larch timber, stone plinths and steep pitched roofs — reinterpreted with contemporary precision. Thirty apartments across six standalone chalets.

A full spa, indoor pool, restaurant and access to the national park trails make Kościelisko the most programme-rich property in the Holistone portfolio.
